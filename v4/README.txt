v4:

This version is a refinement of v3, also fixed 5V with 2 USB charge ports. 

It has a 5.5mm/2.1mm barrel connector on the input, and pads for bare wires
up to 16 AWG on input and output.

The board is compatible with 1cmg.net, 48mm (1.89") square with 3.2mm (1/8") 
mounting holes on 40mm (1.575") centers, suitable for 4-40 or M3 screws.
